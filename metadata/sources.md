# Sources

This file tracks source categories and verification expectations.

## Primary / official source categories

- legislatie.just.ro
- Monitorul Oficial
- ministry or authority pages when they publish official instructions, procedures, or source references
- ISC, ANRE, ISCIR, IGSU, MDLPA or successor authority pages when applicable

## Source rules

- Prefer official or primary sources.
- Do not treat blogs, commentary, commercial legal databases, or AI output as authoritative.
- If a secondary source helps discovery, use it only as a lead and verify against a primary source.
- Record `last_checked` for every act.
- If source availability or reuse rights are unclear, mark the item `unknown` and add a TODO.

## Reuse and copyright

Official legislative, administrative, and judicial texts may be included when sourced from official public sources and when the repository records source URL, official source name, and date checked.

Do not import:

- third-party annotations
- commercial database content
- private summaries
- legal commentary
- paid/legal publisher material
- standards or technical documents unless reuse rights are explicit

If reuse rights are unclear, keep only metadata, source links, relationships, and TODO placeholders until the source policy is verified.
