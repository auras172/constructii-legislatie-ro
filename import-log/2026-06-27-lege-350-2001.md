## Date note

The Portal Legislativ actualizată form at `/DetaliiDocument/184772` shows the law date internally as "6 iunie 2001" which appears to be a data entry error in the portal. The forma de baza at `/DetaliiDocumentAfis/29453` correctly shows "06/07/2001". The law was published in M.Of. nr. 373 din 10 iulie 2001, and the adoption date recorded elsewhere in the text is "7 iunie 2001" (Senat) and "18 iunie 2001" (Camera Deputaților). The title in metadata and frontmatter uses the correct date: "6 iulie 2001" (as the act number date). The official text body is preserved verbatim including the portal's "6 iunie" error.
