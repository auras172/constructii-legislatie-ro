# Normative

This folder is for technical norms, prescriptions, procedures, and related construction standards when source and reuse rights are verified.

MVP status: placeholders only.

## Rules

- Keep one normative act or prescription per file.
- Cite official source and date checked.
- Do not copy full text unless reuse is permitted.
- Preserve numbering and headings when excerpts are permitted.
- Mark uncertain items as `TODO: verify official title/source`.

## Candidate areas

- fire safety norms, including P118 references
- ISCIR prescriptions relevant to construction works and installations
- ANRE technical and authorization references relevant to electrical works
- nZEB and energy efficiency norms
