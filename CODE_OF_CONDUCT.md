# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

Contributors should keep this project respectful, factual, and source-backed.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and precise.
- Cite sources for factual claims.
- Separate facts from interpretation.
- Welcome corrections when evidence improves.
- Keep discussions focused on the repository scope.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment or personal attacks.
- Presenting legal advice as project truth.
- Adding unsourced legal claims.
- Adding copyrighted text without permission.
- Publishing private or sensitive data.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, reject, or remove contributions that violate these rules.
