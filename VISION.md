# Vision

**Open-source, versioned construction legislation for Romania — structured for humans, Git, search, and AI.**

This project does not try to become a new Monitorul Oficial or a replacement for official legal sources. Official sources remain authoritative.

We are building the infrastructure that official portals usually do not provide in a Git-native way:

- Markdown files that humans can read and review.
- Git history for every structural change.
- Diffs between versions of an act.
- Machine-readable metadata for search, validation, and automation.
- Stable source links and import provenance.
- A neutral contribution workflow for developers, engineers, architects, legal researchers, and AI builders.

## Why this exists

Romanian construction regulation is spread across laws, government decisions, ministerial orders, technical norms, authority procedures, and guidance. People who build, design, authorize, inspect, finance, or research construction projects need reliable structure around those sources.

This repository aims to provide that structure without pretending to replace the official text.

## What can be built on top

If the source material is structured consistently, others can build:

- AI legal assistants for construction, with source citations.
- Git diffs between legislative versions.
- Notifications when a relevant act changes.
- Semantic search over construction rules.
- RAG systems for design firms, contractors, consultants, and founders.
- Automated compliance checklists.
- Stable article-level citations.
- Open APIs and datasets for research.
- Teaching material for universities and professional communities.

## Principles

- Official sources first.
- Metadata before interpretation.
- Provenance for every import.
- No legal advice.
- No fake or hallucinated articles.
- No third-party copyrighted annotations unless reuse rights are explicit.
- Small, reviewable contributions.
- Open governance and neutral positioning.

## Relationship to Radar Meșeriași

Radar Meșeriași may consume this project as open infrastructure, but this repository is intentionally neutral and reusable by the wider Romanian ConTech ecosystem.
